What is Tachisme?

Tachisme is a style of art popularized in Europe, particularly in France, around the 1940s and 1950s. Visual depictions of tachisme consist of stains, blobs, and markings. The name itself derives from the French word tache, which means stain. The work itself is spontaneous and abstract.
